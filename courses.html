Enneagram

An online 30-minute questionnaire with coach support during the personal change process.

A dynamic personality framework that identifies nine distinct types, each representing core motivations, fears, and desires. By offering insights into individual and team behaviors, it moves beyond surface-level traits to reveal deeper drivers of actions. It enhances employee engagement, boosts productivity, and supports leadership development by tailoring approaches to unique needs. Additionally, it serves as a tool for change management and cultural transformation, creating cohesive teams and driving long-term organizational success.
